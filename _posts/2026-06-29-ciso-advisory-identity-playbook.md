## Executive Briefing
Modern enterprise perimeters crumble not from direct frontal exploits, but via credential degradation across non-isolated administrative tiers. This playbook outlines immediate architecture postures to limit blast radiuses.

## Strategic Control Imperatives
1. **Enforce Absolute Tier Separation:** Restrict tier-0 credentials from touching lower-integrity assets.
2. **Eliminate Standing Access:** Transition operational tasks entirely to Just-In-Time (JIT) elevation workflows.
